Head of Biostatistics Automation

Help build the automation engine for the future of clinical trial statistics

Idaptiq is building a software platform that automates core components of clinical trial biostatistics and statistical programming. Our system translates clinical trial protocols into structured digital workflows capable of generating downstream statistical artifacts including SAP components, dataset specifications, and analysis outputs.

Role Overview

We are seeking a senior biostatistics or statistical programming expert to lead the development of the domain intelligence behind the Idaptiq platform. This individual will guide the translation of statistical workflows into structured automation logic and manage the subject-matter expert team responsible for developing the automation “skills” powering the system.

Responsibilities

  • Lead the SME team developing biostatistics automation logic
  • Translate statistical workflows into structured automation frameworks
  • Partner closely with engineering to guide product development
  • Ensure alignment with CDISC standards and regulatory expectations
  • Guide development of outputs including SAP text, SDTM/ADaM specifications, and analysis artifacts

Qualifications

  • Deep experience in clinical trial biostatistics or statistical programming
  • Strong familiarity with SAPs, SDTM, ADaM, and CDISC standards
  • Leadership experience managing statistical programming or biostatistics teams
  • Interest in automation, AI, and transforming clinical trial workflows
